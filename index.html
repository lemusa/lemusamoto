An Unexpected Journey

SAMUEL
LEMUSA

Same letters, new adventure.

I’m Sam — a casual adventure rider from Christchurch, NZ, exploring back roads, riverbeds, and rugged tracks on two wheels. I ride a CF Moto 450MT and a Honda CRF250L. The whole thing really started when I tagged along on a ride with the Bad Idea Bros and caught the bug — group rides and the thrill of exploring our country with like-minded mates quickly became a big part of the fun. Now, making videos has become just as much a part of the hobby as the riding itself. This site is where I share my rides, favourite gear, and the story behind LemusaMoto. Still learning as I go — check out the latest videos on YouTube.

The Bikes

  • Torque: 44 Nm

 2024 CFMOTO 450MT

My first new bike, purchased from the first shipment to arrive in NZ in 2024. Fitted with an Uncle Wang bash plate and hand guards, OEM crash bars, Michelin Anakee Wild tyres, and a few AliExpress farkles — luggage plate, reservoir protection, and a tail tidy. In May 2025, it clicked over 5,000 km.
  • Power: 18.2 kW (24.4 hp)
  • Torque: 22 Nm
  • Weight: 150 kg (wet)
  • Range: ~350 km
 2014 Honda CRF250L

Purchased in January 2023, this is my do-anything bike — it gets a hard time but soaks up the abuse. Fitted with Barkbusters hand guards, Motoz RallZ tyres, B&B bash plate, Acerbis 12.5L fuel tank, a gripper seat cover, and a windshield for on-road comfort. Of course, it also wears the compulsory AliExpress bling. With almost 30,000 km on the clock, this bike has been bulletproof.
